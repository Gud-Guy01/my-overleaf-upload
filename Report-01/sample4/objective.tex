\noindent The objective of this shear test are as follows:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item To determine the shear stress ($\tau_{v,d}$) of each timber specimen parallet to the grain.
    \item To compute the mean shear stress ($\tau_m$), standard deviation ($\bar{S}$), and coefficient of variation ($C_v$).
    \item To determine the characteristic shear resistance $f_{v,k}$ using Method 1 (Wood Handbook): $\sigma_{b12,m} = \tau_m - S_m $, then $  f_{v,k} = \sigma_{b12,m} \times p_k$ (for shear $p_k = 14 \%$).
    \item To verify the result using formula: $f_{v,k} = 0.2  f_{m,k}^{0.8}$.
    \item To observe the shear failure mode and compare experimental result with standard design values.
\end{enumerate}
